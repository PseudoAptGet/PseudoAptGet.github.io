Perceivable

For something to be perceivable, everyone must be able to experience it. That's the ultimate motto of the "perceivable" guideline, especially because its recommendations work around bypassing others' physiological limitations (visual or auditory) to make sure they can experience the content to an acceptable degree.

Supplementary Content for Web Media

By supplementary content, the main idea is to have captions for media such as videos or recordings. That way, those that are deaf and/or suffering from hearing loss can experience the content.

Assistive Technology Support

Basic idea here is that for image-based media, there should be text based explanations that a screenreader can instead offer to the visually impaired.
